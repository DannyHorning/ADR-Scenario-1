Analytics

Status

Proposed

Context

As we develop the retail mobile app, it’s crucial to choose an analytics service to track user behavior, app performance, and engagement metrics. Selecting the right analytics service will provide valuable insights to improve the app’s functionality, user experience, and business outcomes.

Decision

Google Analytics for Firebase

Consequences

Google Analytics for Firebase offers comprehensive tracking capabilities, allowing us to monitor various user interactions, events, and screen views within the app.
This includes tracking user engagement, retention, conversion rates, and in-app purchases, providing valuable insights into user behavior and app performance. Google Analytics for Firebase offers a generous free tier with unlimited event reporting and up to 500 distinct user properties. As the app scales and data volume increases, Google Analytics for Firebase provides flexible pricing options and scalability to accommodate growing analytics needs.
